Keep In Shape

Why Should You?

Keeping in shape and looking after your physical and mental health will make you perform better on the server. It will make you a better gamer and improve performance. Having a clear mind will allow you to focus on the game ahead of you. Having a healthy mind and body will make you happier.

The Research

It has been documented many times by various studies and universities that physical activity improves mood, health and mindset. Which all three things are very important for performance in gaming. Having the right mindset and mood will influence your performance and make you a better player.

How To Keep In Shape

Early Steps

If you aren't a massive fan of physical activity and working at a gym. Getting up from your monitor screen often to go for a walk and breathe some fresh air is a good step towards it. Helping clear your mind and relax. It will get the blood flowing in your legs and allows the mind to clear for the next game.

Next Steps

Once you get used to going for walks and getting up often. Try devoting 45 minutes to an hour of your day to exercise. You can step it up from a walk. For example, getting a gym membership, going for a run, lifting weights, bike ride are all good ways to clear the mind and work on your physical self.
