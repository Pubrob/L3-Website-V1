Practice Your Aim

Train Your Aim

Games like CSGO, Valorant, Call Of Duty FPS games core mechanic is the shooting. Being able to consistently aim at a high level will give you a massive edge over your opponents. As being able to point and click on your opponent faster than them means you kill them faster and quicker than they can.

The Map

A custom map made for CSGO called Aim_botz is a great place to start practising your aim. With many settings within the map it allows the user to set up the bots as they like. With any weapon of choice. It is a great map for practising switching targets and getting consistent headshot kills and sprays.

Practice Your Maps

Map Knowledge

Map Knowledge is an important in competitive multiplayer games like CSGO, Valorant and even MOBA games like Dota 2 and League Of Legends. Before queuing for new maps do some research and learn the maps, its positions, callouts and tactics to make you the best player you can be and win.

Learning The Maps

The best way to learn a new map is to load into a private match and walk around the map. Getting a grasp of how the map looks sightlines and details. Once you have an idea of the lanes, doing research of pro matches and YouTube videos will allow you to know what pros do on that map to be the best.
